What is the core advantage of the Transformer architecture?

smart_toy

The core advantage of the Transformer architecture, as detailed in the seminal paper, lies in its reliance entirely on attention mechanisms to draw global dependencies between input and output. Unlike recurrent neural networks (RNNs) or convolutional neural networks (CNNs), which process data sequentially or hierarchically, Transformers process all tokens simultaneously.

This enables significantly more parallelization during training, leading to faster convergence and the ability to train on vastly larger datasets. Furthermore, the self-attention mechanism allows the model to inherently learn contextual relationships across long distances within the sequence, mitigating the vanishing gradient problem common in earlier sequential models.
